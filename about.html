Our Story

We create fun, eco-friendly fidget toys that help with focus, stress relief, and everyday satisfaction — for kids and adults alike.

Our Mission

Everyday Satisfaction

At Fidget Street, we believe everyone deserves access to high-quality fidget toys that are fun, safe, and eco-friendly. Our toys help with focus, reduce stress, and provide endless satisfaction.

Every toy we sell is carefully selected for quality and safety. Made from plant-based PLA plastic, our products are kind to the planet while providing hours of fidgety fun for ages 6 and up.

We're not just selling toys — we're building a community of fidget enthusiasts who appreciate quality, sustainability, and the simple joy of a satisfying click or spin.

The Journey

How It All Started

Fidget Street started with a simple observation: fidget toys aren't just for kids with ADHD — they're for everyone who needs to destress, focus, or simply enjoy something satisfying in their hands.

We noticed how a simple click, spin, or squeeze could transform a stressful moment into a calm one. Whether it's a student studying for exams or an adult in a long meeting, fidget toys have the power to help.

We're passionate about sustainability, which is why we focus on eco-friendly products made from plant-based PLA plastic. Good toys shouldn't cost the earth — literally.

Every product in our collection is carefully tested for quality and safety. We only stock toys that we'd be happy to give to our own families — durable, safe for ages 6+, and endlessly satisfying.

Today, Fidget Street serves fidget fans across the UK. Whether you're looking for articulated toys, pop-its, fidget cubes, or clicker toys, we've got something for everyone.

Thank you for being part of the Fidget Street crew!
