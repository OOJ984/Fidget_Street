Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our products, orders, shipping, and more.

Browse our collection and click the 'Buy Now' button on any product. This will take you to the trusted Wicka Trading Station where you can complete your purchase securely.

We process orders quickly, so please contact us as soon as possible if you need to make changes. Email us at wicka@protonmail.com with your order number. If your order hasn't been dispatched yet, we'll do our best to accommodate your request.

We accept all major credit and debit cards through our secure payment system. At events and markets, we also accept cash and may offer additional payment options. All online transactions are encrypted and secure.

UK orders typically arrive within 3-5 working days. Orders are dispatched within 1-2 business days of being placed. During busy periods (holidays, sales), delivery may take slightly longer. You'll receive tracking information once your order ships.

Yes! We offer free UK shipping on all orders over £30. Orders under £30 have a flat shipping rate of £2.99. This helps us keep our prices affordable while ensuring your items arrive safely.

Currently, we only ship within the UK and Ireland. We're working on expanding our shipping options in the future. If you're outside these areas and really want our products, contact us and we'll see what we can arrange!

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery for items in their original, unworn condition with all packaging. Due to hygiene reasons, organisers cannot be returned if the packaging has been opened, unless they're faulty. Please see our full Returns Policy for details.

We're so sorry if this happens! Please contact us within 48 hours of receiving your order with photos of the damage. We'll arrange a replacement or full refund as quickly as possible. Your satisfaction is our priority.

Yes! All our organisers are made with 316 surgical steel, which is nickel-free and hypoallergenic. If you're allergic to normal organisers, these won't irritate your ears.

Our storage solutions are made from PLA (polylactic acid), a biodegradable thermoplastic derived from renewable resources like corn starch. We source our filament from UK suppliers and design all earring-organiser in-house. They're sturdy, lightweight, and eco-friendlier than traditional plastics.

We love hearing your ideas! While we can't always accommodate every request, we're open to custom orders when time and materials allow. Contact us with your idea and we'll let you know if we can make it happen. Custom orders may have additional costs and longer lead times.

Store your organisers in a safe place, like one of our storage solutions, to maintain shine and durability. Avoid contact with perfumes, lotions, and other chemicals. With proper care, your Wicka pieces will last for years!

Our displays are 3D-printed with strong, high-quality materials, making them sturdy, long-lasting, and perfect for everyday use.

Yes, all our items are designed and created in the UK. We get all our supplies from the UK too!
